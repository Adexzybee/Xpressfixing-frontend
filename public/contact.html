Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a repair take?
Most repairs are completed within 24 hours. Express service is available for same-day repairs for an additional fee.
Do you offer a warranty?
Yes! All repairs come with a 14-day warranty. If anything goes wrong, we'll fix it for free.
How do I pay?
We accept card payments, bank transfers, and Paystack. Payment is made after you approve the engineer's quote.
Do you pick up and deliver?
Yes! Our dispatch team picks up your device and delivers it back once repaired. Delivery is free within our service areas.
🗺️ Service Areas: Lagos (Lekki, VI, Ikeja, Surulere), Abuja (Wuse, Garki, Maitama), Port Harcourt (GRA, Rumuokoro)
